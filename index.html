Implement a sequential version of a two-dimensional predator-prey model with spatial diffusion using C++. The model determines the interaction of pumas (predator) and hares (prey) in a two-dimensional landscape. Pumas eat hares. Without hares, pumas decline and die out through lack of food. Without pumas, hares breed and increase in numbers forever.
